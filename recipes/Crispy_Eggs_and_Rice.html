Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 Cup cooked white rice
  • Soy sauce
  • Spinach
  • Sesame Oil

Steps

  1. Coat the pan with sesame oil and set to medium heat
  2. Add 3 eggs to the pan and fry until the edges of the egg turn dark golden brown
  3. Assemble rice and eggs onto a plate
  4. Wash and chop spinach and add it to plate
  5. Drizzle soy sauce and enjoy
